Welcome to Tariq Anwar Projects

Rich Experience of Construction

Tariq Anwar Projects is an Indian company registered with CPWD incorporated in 2023. The company offers Engineering and Construction services. Tariq Anwar Projects is dedicated to organizations or Individuals seeking construction services. We provide engineering construction services to CPWD, CPSE, State PWDs, state municipalities, and commercial/ residential real estate developers. Our area of expertise includes but not limited to building technology, civil and structural engineering.

Learn More

We are committed to providing the best possible construction expertise & service to ensure cost effective and successful projects. We work closely with our clients to accurately interpret their dreams/visions in drawings and bring them to the desired reality through construction solutions to meet their needs; We communicate with our clients throughout the construction process to keep them informed of progress and to ensure that our project keeps on schedule and within budget.

Director's Message

We are delighted to extend a warm welcome to you from our distinguished civil construction firm, proudly registered with the Central Public Works Department (CPWD). With a legacy of excellence and a profound understanding of project design and execution, we stand as a beacon of quality and innovation in the construction industry. At our core, we believe that construction is an art that merges science, technology, and creativity. Our team comprises seasoned professionals who bring a wealth of experience in the realm of civil engineering. With each project we undertake, we weave together expertise, precision, and a commitment to turning architectural dreams into tangible reality.
